## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**"What should I order?"**

*For Brunch:* The Butter Chicken Benny is the most famous dish—people come specifically for it. The pancakes are stunning and shareable. For something more traditionally Indian, try Chole Kulcha.

*For Dinner:* Start with the Heavenly Black Lentils (slow-cooked 12 hours). Butter Chicken is a crowd-pleaser. Menu is designed for sharing—get 3-4 dishes for two people.

**"Is it authentic Indian food?"**

Both authentic and modern. Chef from Mumbai uses traditional techniques with contemporary approach. Not a "curry house"—showcasing real diversity of Indian cuisine.

**"Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?"**

Absolutely. Extensive vegetarian and vegan menus (it's a specialty). Can handle gluten-free, halal, and most allergies. Note when booking and remind server on arrival.

**"Do I need a reservation?"**

Yes, highly recommended, especially weekends and brunch. Small space fills up quickly. Same-day bookings often difficult for popular times.

**"Where are you located?"**

Van der Helstplein 15H in De Pijp neighborhood. Beautiful square, 20-min walk from Museumplein, near Albert Cuyp Market. Easy to find.

**"How long does dinner take?"**

Most guests spend 1.5-2 hours, but you're welcome to take your time. Shared plates let you control the pace.

**"Can I book the whole restaurant?"**

For private events or buyouts, connect with events team for details.

**"Do you do takeaway or delivery?"**

Yes, but experience is optimized for dining in. Ambiance, fresh preparation, presentation are all part of what makes it special. Takeaway available if needed.

**"What's the best time to visit?"**

For relaxed experience: weekday dinners or early brunch. For full energy: weekend evenings. Depends on what you're looking for.

**"Do you have a bar?"**

Yes! Beautiful hand-crafted bar with stone and banana fiber lighting. Delicious cocktails.

**"What language is spoken?"**

Primarily English. Multi-cultural team from all parts of the world. Language of warm hospitality.

**"Can I bring my dog?"**

Yes, on the heated terrace! For hygiene/safety, pets stay outside indoor areas.

**"Do you sell gift vouchers?"**

Yes, available for purchase online through the website.

**"Do you offer group reservations or catering?"**

Yes, for corporate events, family gatherings, special celebrations. Contact directly for event packages.

**"What is your lunch concept?"**

Indian take on classic brunch dishes. Friday-Sunday. Traditional breakfast/lunch favorites infused with Indian flavors.
